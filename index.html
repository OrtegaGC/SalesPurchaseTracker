SalesPurchaseTracker

Sales Purchase Tracker software enables user to manage his business's sales & purchase. It maintains stock, saves bills, calculates profit-loss and VAT.

Welcome to Sales Purchase tracker.

SalesPurchaseTracker makes your life easy by providing an user friendly way of managing your business's sales & purchase and VAT calculation. you can check out the new branch:

$ cd sayan801/SalesPurchaseTracker
$ git fetch origin
$ git checkout SalesPurchaseTracker

Quick Start Guide

Sales Purchase Tracker software enables user to manage his business's sales & purchase. It maintains stock, saves bills, calculates profit-loss and VAT. More detailed user guide coming soon.

Features

  • sales billing
  • purchase billing
  • sales list
  • purchase list
  • stock management
  • saves bills
  • calculates profit-loss
  • VAT calculation
  • GST calculation
